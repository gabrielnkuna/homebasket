## Icon Concept
The icon is a unified geometric shape: a wide trapezoid basket body topped by a triangular house roofline. The roof (gold) and basket (green/white) share a single seamless silhouette. Four vertical rounded slots suggest a woven basket. A small gold circle at the roof apex adds a premium finishing detail.

## Typography
- Font family: Nunito / Poppins / Helvetica Neue (rounded sans-serif)
- "Home" — bold weight, forest green
- "BASKET" — semi-bold, spaced caps, warm gold
