# Play Store Prompt Pack

Use these prompts in Nano Bana or your preferred design/image tool.

## 1. Feature graphic prompt

```text
Create a Google Play feature graphic for the mobile app “Home Basket”.

Canvas:
- 1024 x 500
- no transparency
- Android Play Store ready

Brand feel:
- premium, warm, organized, household-focused
- deep forest green base
- muted gold and sand accents
- clean modern mobile-product aesthetic

Concept:
- one shared household shopping app
- represent shared list, purchases, and pay-day budget tracking
- subtle basket-and-home brand cues
- global feel, suitable for in-store, online, and delivery shopping

Composition:
- left side: clear headline area and subtle brand lockup
- right side: refined mobile UI crops or elegant device mockups
- layered cards showing list, purchases, and budget cycle
- soft depth and glow
- no tiny unreadable detail

Text:
- headline: “Shared shopping for the whole household”
- supporting line: “Stay in sync across family, roommates, and helpers”

Avoid:
- grocery flyer look
- loud supermarket imagery
- shopping cart clichés
- repetitive oversized app icon
- purple-heavy palettes
- cluttered collage
- tiny unreadable UI

Output:
- polished, premium, trustworthy Play Store feature graphic
```

## 2. Screenshot frame styling prompt

```text
Create a clean Android Play Store screenshot styling system for the app “Home Basket”.

Style:
- portrait mobile app store presentation
- premium productivity app aesthetic
- deep forest green, warm gold, and soft neutral palette
- simple top headline area
- real app UI centered and readable
- elegant spacing and rounded cards

Text styling:
- large bold heading only
- no paragraphs
- high readability at small size

Avoid:
- fake 3D phone frames unless very subtle
- busy gradients
- generic fintech look
- childish illustrations

Output:
- consistent screenshot template system for 6 screenshots
```
