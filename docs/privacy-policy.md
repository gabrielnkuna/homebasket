## What Home Basket does

Home Basket is a shared household shopping and purchase tracking app. It lets household members manage a shared list, record purchases, upload receipt images, set reminders, and optionally link an account for restoration across devices.

## Information we collect

Home Basket may collect and store the following categories of information:

- Account information, such as your email address, if you choose to link an account with email and password.
- Authentication identifiers generated through Firebase Authentication.
- Household information, such as household names, member names, invite codes, and role information.
- Shopping and purchase information, such as list items, reminders, notes, purchase totals, sellers or stores, and purchase history.
- Receipt images and receipt-related metadata that you upload.
- Technical and diagnostic information needed to operate the service, such as device-linked authentication data and service logs.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- provide the Home Basket service
- sync household data across devices
- authenticate users and restore linked household sessions
- store purchases and receipt records
- support reminders and budgeting features
- maintain security, reliability, and troubleshooting
- respond to support and deletion requests

## How data is stored and processed

Home Basket uses third-party infrastructure providers to operate core features, including Firebase services such as:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Cloud Storage

These services may process and store data on infrastructure operated by Google or its service providers.

## How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in these limited ways:

- with service providers that help us operate Home Basket
- with other members of the same household, where the data is part of that shared household experience
- when required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
- when necessary to protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of users, the service, or the publisher

## Data retention

We keep data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain household continuity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

If you request deletion, we will use reasonable efforts to remove or anonymize data associated with your request, subject to legal, security, fraud-prevention, and operational retention needs.

## Account and data deletion

If you linked an account to Home Basket and want your account or data deleted, contact:

- gabriel@transcripe.com

Please include enough information for us to identify your account safely.

Where household data is shared across multiple members, some records may remain visible to other household members if they are part of the shared household history, unless broader deletion is specifically requested and feasible.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including authenticated access controls and managed cloud infrastructure. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International users

If you use Home Basket outside the country where the service is operated, your data may be transferred to and processed in other countries where our service providers operate.

## Children

Home Basket is not directed to children without appropriate supervision where required by applicable law.
